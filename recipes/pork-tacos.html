Ground Pork Tacos with Pineapple Salsa

Description

It's always good to try something new ... like ground pok instead of beef in your tacos! The flavour is somewhat reminiscent of al pastor tacos, but ready in a fraction of the time from scratch.

Ingredients

Tacos:

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 tablespoon soy-based liquid seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon chipotle powder
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 8 small/medium tortillas

Pineapple Salsa:

  • 1 cup diced fresh pineapple
  • 1/4 cup minced red onion
  • 1 small jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh basil
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Combine ground pork, liquid seasoning, ground chipotle powder, and garlic in a bowl. Set aside for flavours to meld.
  2. Combine pineapple, red onion, jalapeno, basil, lime juice, and salt in a bowl. Set pineapple salsa aside.
  3. Preheat a large skillet over high heat. Crumble ground pork into the hot skillet. Cook and stir until pork is completely browned and edges begin to crisp, 7 to 10 minutes. Drain any excess grease. Place pork in a bowl and keep warm. Wipe out skillet.
  4. Warm tortillas in skillet over medium-low heat, about 20 seconds per side. Divide meat mixture between tortillas and top with pineapple salsa.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

Cook's Note:

If you cannot find any soy-based liquid seasoning, you can combine equal parts soy sauce and Worcestershire sauce.
